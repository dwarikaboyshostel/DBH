About Dwarika Boys Hostel

Your Trusted Home Away From Home Since 2019

Our Story

Dwarika Boys Hostel was founded with a simple yet powerful vision - to provide students and working professionals with a comfortable, safe, and affordable place to call home. What started as a small family initiative has grown into one of Muzaffarpur's most trusted hostel facilities.

Our journey began in 2019 when we recognized the need for quality accommodation that goes beyond just providing a place to stay. We wanted to create an environment where students could focus on their studies while enjoying the comforts of home.

Meet Our Founder

Kumari Roma Sinha

Founder & Managing Director

Roma Sinha, the heart and soul behind Dwarika Boys Hostel, personally oversees every aspect of our operations. With her motherly care and attention to detail, she ensures that every resident feels at home.

The Story Behind Our Name

VIJAY SUDHA is the name of our small, home-based business, lovingly chosen in memory of our late father-in-law and mother-in-law, who will always remain in our hearts. Dwarika Boys Hostel is a unit of VIJAY SUDHA, personally operated by Kumari Roma Sinha. The name "Dwarika" holds deep spiritual meaning for us, as it reflects our strong faith and devotion to Bhagwan (God).
